FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does tailoring and installation take?

It typically takes 3 to 7 working days depending on order size. For urgent orders, we can deliver within 48 hours.

Is the home visit really free?

Yes, the home visit for measuring and consultation is 100% free throughout Riyadh with no obligation to purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, Mada, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, and STC Pay.

Do you offer a warranty on products?

Yes, we offer a comprehensive warranty on fabrics, installation, and after-sales service for one full year.

What types of curtains do you offer?

We offer blackout, roller, sheer, classic, and wooden curtains. We also provide imported fabrics in various designs and colors.
